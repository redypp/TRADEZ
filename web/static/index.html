Break & Retest — Signal Methods

All logic runs on 15-minute MES candles. The strategy uses a state machine: NEUTRAL → WATCHING → ENTRY (or timeout back to NEUTRAL).

STATE MACHINE
NEUTRAL
Scanning all levels
every 15-min bar
break detected
+ volume + body
→
WATCHING LONG
Awaiting bullish
retest candle
WATCHING SHORT
Awaiting bearish
retest candle
all criteria
confirmed
→
ENTRY
Signal fired
SL + TP set
timeout / invalidated
↩
L
Level Priority
4 level types, checked in order
1
VWAP
Volume-weighted avg price · resets daily at open · highest confluence
2
PDH / PDL
Previous day high & low · institutional reference prices
3
ORH / ORL
Opening range high & low · first 30 min (9:30–10:00 ET)
4
SWING
Rolling N-bar high & low · dynamic support & resistance
B
Break Detection
_detect_brt_signals() — all 3 must pass
✓
Candle close
Close crosses level ± BRT_BREAK_BUFFER × ATR (regime sets break buffer)
✓
Volume confirmation
Volume > BRT_VOLUME_THRESHOLD × vol_ma(20) — requires above-avg participation
✓
Break candle body
|close − open| > break_body_min × ATR — no doji / indecision breaks
R
Retest Criteria
All 6 conditions required for entry
✓
In retest zone
Price touches [level ± tolerance × ATR] — must revisit the broken level
✓
Candle direction
Bullish (close > open) for long · Bearish (open > close) for short
✓
Close confirmation
Close clearly above (long) or below (short) the broken level
✓
EMA 20 alignment
Long: close > EMA20  |  Short: close < EMA20
✓
ADX threshold
ADX > adx_min (regime-adaptive: 20 → 25) — confirming trend strength
✓
RSI range
Long: RSI 35–75  |  Short: RSI 25–65 — avoids extreme overextension
$
Stop Loss & Take Profit
ATR-based, regime-adaptive sizing
SL
Stop Loss (Long)
min(candle_low, level) − sl_buffer × ATR
SL
Stop Loss (Short)
max(candle_high, level) + sl_buffer × ATR
TP
Take Profit (both)
entry ± (entry − stop) × tp_rr · R:R is regime-adaptive (1.5R – 2.5R)
R
Risk per trade
Contracts sized by account risk % / (entry − SL × $5/pt MES multiplier)
T
Session Rules
Time filters applied before entry
✓
Session window
Entries only between 10:00 – 15:00 ET · avoids open auction and late illiquidity
✓
Scheduler cadence
Runs at :02 past each hour · checks last completed 15-min bar
✓
Daily loss limit
Max 2 losses per day · trading halts if daily P&L < −$200
✓
Max daily trades
Max 3 trades per session · prevents overtrading in choppy tape
I
Indicator Calculations
prepare_break_retest() output columns
ATR
Average True Range
14-period · scales all buffer calculations to current volatility
EMA
Exponential MA (20)
Trend direction filter · price above = bullish bias
ADX
Avg Directional Index
14-period · measures trend strength, not direction
RSI
Relative Strength Index
14-period · momentum filter, keeps out extreme mean-reversion zones
VOL
Volume MA (20)
20-bar rolling average · baseline for break volume confirmation
VWAP
Intraday VWAP
Resets daily at market open · anchored to session cumulative vol
